Dice Game

Rules:

  1. Your goal is to roll each number exactly one time.
  2. You have two attempts to roll a unique number. You can click a die to freeze it between attempts if you want. [Hint: you can freeze both and click roll to keep first attempt.]
  3. The game ends if your second roll is a repeat number or if you make it to the end and win!

Roll the dice to begin.
