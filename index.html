dice game

rules:

  1. Your goal is to complete stages 1-11 by rolling a unique number each time.
  2. At each stage, you have two attempts to roll a unique number. You can click a die to freeze it between attempts if you want.
  3. The game ends if your final roll is a repeat number or if you make it to the end and win!
